1.0 Background of study

Online Payment due generating system is a viable source in any Schools, departments and organization, its main purpose is to maintain more reliable records of money received in and how it is spent. According to ENIAC (1946) “Online Payment due allow the user to make their payment without coming to school.

Online payment due generating system mainly focuses on a simple interface for storing and analyzing students dues considering various aspects including Academic, Achievements, Sincerity and other parameters. The Data can be retrieved in Statistical form which aids in Performance and Progress of the department in the Institution. It can be used by educational institutions to manage the records easily. The management of accurate and up-to-date information regarding a students’ academic career is critically important in the colleges.  Departmental due generating system deals with all kinds of students record.
